Transformation & Agentic AI

The bottleneck in deploying AI inside regulated institutions is not the technology. It is the ability to identify where a capability belongs inside an operating model, convert it into a governed use case with a quantified outcome, and scale capacity without proportional workforce growth. Governance is designed before any agent is built.

AI Transformation Approach · Ongoing

Agentic AI is new and the field is still in discovery. The approach below is live and evolving: converting emerging AI capability into commercial outcomes inside regulated institutions, with every engagement scoped around measurable value, governed before it is built, and designed for production scale from the outset.

Commercial Use Case Design
Scoping agentic AI use cases against high-value operational problems with quantified commercial outcomes: effort reduction, cycle time compression and elimination of manual processing at scale. Each use case is built with a full benefit realisation model before a line of code is written, and a business case designed for MD-level approval. Revenue growth comes from the capacity freed by intelligent automation.
Future-State Target Operating Models
Designing target states where agentic pipelines absorb volume growth, new regulatory obligations and market complexity without proportional workforce expansion. The target is a production-grade operating model embedded into day-to-day operations, not an isolated pilot. AI is built into the business architecture from the outset, not retrofitted.
Governance as Deployment Condition
In regulated institutions, AI deployability is a governance problem before it is a technology problem. Autonomy taxonomies, MRM frameworks and audit trail design are completed before any agent is built. This produces use cases that governance forums can approve, developers can build and regulators can audit: the conditions for moving from POC to production.
From POC to Enterprise Scale
Building governed POCs ahead of enterprise platform availability. The FATCA/CRS agentic pipeline was designed, governed and evidenced before Citi Arc launched in April 2026; when the platform arrived, enterprise adoption became a deployment decision rather than a new project. The model: design, govern and evidence the use case so it is production-ready the moment the platform layer exists.
Embedding AI into Delivery Practice
AI adoption happens at the programme level, not the platform layer. Coaching MD and Director-level stakeholders on agentic design principles, embedding AI lenses into programme charters and assessing existing RPA estates for agent replacement. Every programme is evaluated for its AI potential as discovery continues across the industry.
The 6-Step Transformation Path

A structured, repeatable approach for deploying agentic AI inside regulated institutions. Sequential and deliberate: governance designed before code is written, value evidenced before scale is approved.

01 / IDENTIFY
Find the Right Process
High volume, rule-bound, structured data inputs and measurable outputs are the hallmarks of a strong agentic candidate. Quantify current-state FTE, cycle time, error rates and manual handoffs.
Current-state process mapping
02 / DESIGN
Define the Target Operating Model
Map agent responsibilities, data flows, HITL thresholds and the boundary between automated decisions and human judgement. The TOM is the deliverable, not the code.
Agent role and boundary design
03 / GOVERN
Build Governance Before Code
Establish the autonomy taxonomy, MRM framework and audit trail design before writing a line of code. Governance is the condition for deployment inside any regulated institution.
MRM framework · Autonomy taxonomy
04 / BUILD
Develop the Agentic Pipeline
Document intelligence, multi-agent orchestration, HITL validation layer. Prove the architecture and surface edge cases at POC stage before committing to production scale.
n8n · Claude Code · Orchestration
05 / EVIDENCE
Quantify the Business Case
Model cycle time reduction, effort reduction and exception rates from POC data. Secure executive funding based on evidence from the pipeline, not projection from a slide deck.
Benefits modelling · Exec approval
06 / SCALE
Deploy at Enterprise Scale
Move from POC to enterprise production via the platform layer. Maintain governance integrity, absorb volume growth without proportional headcount, and embed into ongoing delivery.

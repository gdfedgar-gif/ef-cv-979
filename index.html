The Transformation Method · Live & Evolving

A six-step framework applied across the CCR portfolio, Tax Operating Model and Middle East licensing programme at Citi. Designed for regulated institutions where transformation requires governance as much as ambition.

01
Diagnose
Map the AS-IS operating model, identify process pain points, quantify inefficiency and assess organisational readiness for transformation.
Applied at Citi Process impact analysis for the CCR portfolio and Tax Operating Model, including value stream mapping to surface manual touchpoints and quantify savings potential.
02
Architect
Design the Target Operating Model and future-state blueprint across People, Process and Technology, anchored in HOBA business architecture principles.
Applied at Citi Designed the Global Tax TOM across three workstreams; architected the CCR strategic roadmap spanning PFE, Settlement Risk, Stress Testing and Collateral.
03
Align
Secure C-suite sponsorship and build cross-functional stakeholder coalitions, applying ADKAR to translate strategic intent into funded, prioritised programmes.
Applied at Citi Secured MD and Director-level approval for the $10.1M Tax TOM and $15M CCR portfolio despite competing internal priorities.
04
Automate
Re-evaluate legacy RPA and coded integrations against agentic AI capability, identifying where agents outperform previous automation approaches and building the business case.
Applied at Citi Built AI regulatory gap analysis reducing review from a full day to approximately 10 minutes per regulation; modelled FATCA/CRS agentic validation pipeline reducing a 2-day cycle to roughly one hour.
05
Deliver
Govern phased programme delivery through structured PMO cadence, milestone tracking, risk and issue management, and executive steering updates.
Applied at Citi Governed 4-year Global Tax TOM delivery and multi-year CCR strategic roadmap, managing programme teams across concurrent workstreams with up to 30 people in delivery.
06
Realise
Measure and validate benefits, embed capability into business-as-usual and advance the organisation toward continuous improvement and future-ready operations.
Applied at Citi Delivered $5.7M in annual savings from Tax TOM; achieved 70% manual touchpoint reduction; established ongoing governance for CCR issue remediation and risk monitoring.
